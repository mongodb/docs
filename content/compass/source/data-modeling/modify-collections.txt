Modify Collections in Your |compass-short| |erd-title|

About this Task 
---------------

In the :guilabel:`Data Modeling` tab, you can:

- Add or remove collections
- Rename collections

.. include:: /includes/shared/fact-diagram-modification-data.rst

Before You Begin
----------------

You must have an existing |erd| in |compass-short| to modify the 
fields. To create a new diagram, see 
:ref:`compass-create-data-model-diagram`.

Steps
-----

Add a New Collection 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To add a new collection to your |erd|:

.. procedure:: 
   :style: normal 

   .. include:: /includes/shared/steps-data-modeling-open.rst

   .. step:: Add a new collection

      To add a new collection, click the :icon-lg:`Plus` icon in the upper-left
      corner of the diagram canvas. A new collection node appears in the 
      diagram.

      .. image:: /images/compass/dm-add-collection.png
         :alt: Add collection icon in the Data Modeling tab 
         :width: 300px

Rename a Collection 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To rename a collection in your |erd|:

.. procedure:: 
   :style: normal

   .. include:: /includes/shared/steps-data-modeling-open.rst
    
   .. include:: /includes/shared/steps-collection-side-panel.rst

    .. step:: Rename the collection

       Under the :guilabel:`Name` section, specify a new name for your field. 

Remove a Collection
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To delete a collection from your diagram:

.. procedure:: 
   :style: normal 

   .. include:: /includes/shared/steps-data-modeling-open.rst
    
   .. include:: /includes/shared/steps-collection-side-panel.rst

   .. step:: Delete the collection

      In the side panel, click the :icon-lg:`Trash` icon.
