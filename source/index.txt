|service-fullname| is a cloud service for running, monitoring, and
maintaining MongoDB deployments, including the provisioning of
dedicated servers for the MongoDB instances.

MongoDB Deployments
-------------------

MongoDB deployments, or "clusters" in |service|, can be either a
:term:`sharded cluster` or a :term:`replica set`. |service| uses
MongoDB 3.2 with the WiredTiger storage engine.

Security
--------

|service| secures your deployments through the use of TLS/SSL, IP
whitelists, and user authentication and authorization.
