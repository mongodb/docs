===============================
Reopen a Locked Project Account
===============================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

Overview
--------

|mms| will lock a organization's account if the associated credit card
expires.

Reopen an Account
-----------------

If you have received a message that your account is locked for failure
to pay, please update your credit card information.

#. Select your organization in the :guilabel:`Context` dropdown.

#. Click :guilabel:`Settings`, then :guilabel:`Billing Overview`.

#. In the :guilabel:`Payment Methods` section, click :guilabel:`Edit`.

#. Update the information as needed and click :guilabel:`Submit`.
