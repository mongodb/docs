.. setting:: brs.snapshotSchedule.interval

   *Type*: integer

   *Default*: 24

   
   Specifies the time, in hours, between two consecutive snapshots.
   
   Accepted values are:
   
   ``6``, ``8``, ``12``, or ``24``
   
   
   Corresponds to :setting:`Snapshot Interval (Hours) <Snapshot Interval>`
