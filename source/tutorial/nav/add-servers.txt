This section describes how to add servers for use by |mms|
:ref:`automation` or |mms| :ref:`monitoring-functional-overview`.

Monitoring provides deployment metrics, visualization, and alerting on key
database and hardware indicators. Automation provides all Monitoring
functionality and lets you deploy, configure, and update your MongoDB
processes directly from |mms|.
