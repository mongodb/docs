Remove Project from Automation
==============================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

When the |mms| Free Trial period expires, |mms| Automation is disabled
for your |mms| Projects. As Automation no longer manages your MongoDB
processes, you need to remove your MongoDB processes from Automation
then configure them to use Monitoring and Backup without Automation.
