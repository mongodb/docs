   .. step:: Grant temporary access.
      
      a. Locate the {+database-deployment+} to which to grant access 
         from the list of {+database-deployments+}.
      
      #. Select the ellipsis icon (:guilabel:`...`) to the right of the 
         {+database-deployment+}.
      
      #. Select :guilabel:`Grant Temporary Sync Data Access to MongoDB Support`.
      
         When you successfully grant temporary access to MongoDB 
         Support, a new item appears in the dropdown that informs you 
         when your access expires.
