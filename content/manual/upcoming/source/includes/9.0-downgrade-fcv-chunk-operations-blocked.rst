.. note::

   When you downgrade the {+fcv+} from 9.0, MongoDB moves metadata from
   the shards back to the config server. During this transition, the
   {+fcv+} downgrade drains and blocks chunk operations, including:

   - :dbcommand:`split`
   - :dbcommand:`mergeChunks`
   - :dbcommand:`mergeAllChunksOnShard`
   - Migrations, manual and balancer-issued

   Chunk operations resume after the {+fcv+} downgrade completes.
