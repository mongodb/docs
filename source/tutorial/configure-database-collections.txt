*Estimated completion time: 15 minutes*

When you first connect to your {+dl+}, |service| creates a default 
storage configuration that maps data stored in your |s3| buckets to a set 
of databases and collections. This part of the tutorial walks you through 
verifying your databases and collections in the default storage 
configuration. 

Prerequisites
-------------

To complete this part of the tutorial, you will need to have completed:

- **Part 1:** :doc:`Upload sample data to your S3 bucket </tutorial/load-sample-data>`
- **Part 2:** :doc:`/tutorial/deploy`
- **Part 3:** :doc:`/tutorial/connect`
