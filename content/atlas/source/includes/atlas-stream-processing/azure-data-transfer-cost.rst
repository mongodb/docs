{+azure+} Data Transfer Costs for {+atlas-sp+}
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

{+atlas-sp+} data transfer costs depend on the relationship between
the origin region and the destination.

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :stub-columns: 1
   :widths: 40 20

   * - Data Transfer Type
     - Price per GB

   * - Same Region
     - No charge

   * - Same Continent
     - $0.02 to $0.16

   * - Different Continent
     - $0.05 to $0.16

   * - Out to Internet
     - $0.087 to $0.181
