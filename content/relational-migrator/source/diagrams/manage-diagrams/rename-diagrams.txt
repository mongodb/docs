================
Rename a Diagram
================

.. meta::
   :description: Rename diagrams in Relational Migrator by updating the name in the Diagram Properties pane on the Mapping screen.

.. contents:: On this page
   :local: 
   :backlinks: none 
   :depth: 1 
   :class: singlecol

You can rename diagrams on the :guilabel:`Mapping` screen. Relational
Migrator displays diagram names on the Diagram Toolbar. 

About this Task 
---------------

- You can rename diagrams to customize your diagram properties. 
- {+rel-mig+} displays your diagram names on the Diagram Toolbar
  of the :guilabel:`Mapping` screen and in the :guilabel:`Diagram
  Properties` pane. 

Steps 
-----

.. procedure::
   :style:  connected

   .. step:: Navigate to the diagram you want to rename

      In the Diagram Toolbar, click the name of the diagram to display the
      diagram. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram-toolbar.png
         :alt: Diagram toolbar    
   
   .. step:: Open the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane

      Click the :icon-fa5:`chevron-circle-right` arrow button to open
      the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram-properties.png
         :alt: Diagram Properties pane
      
      The :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane displays the active
      diagram name and notes. By default, {+rel-mig+} names
      diagrams in the order they were created and leaves the notes field
      empty. 

   .. step:: Enter your new diagram name in the Name box

      When you click outside the Name box, {+rel-mig+}
      automatically saves your new diagram name and updates the Diagram
      Toolbar.
