.. step:: In |cloud|, go to the :guilabel:`Linked Organizations` page for your organization.
      
   a. If it's not already displayed, select your desired organization 
      from the |ui-org-menu| in the navigation bar.

   #. In the sidebar, click :guilabel:`Linked Organizations` under the 
      :guilabel:`Billing` heading.

      .. note::

         The :guilabel:`Linked Organizations` option displays only if 
         your selected organization is eligible to be a paying 
         organization for cross-organization billing.
      
      The `Linked Organizations <https://cloud.mongodb.com/go?l=https%3A%2F%2Fcloud.mongodb.com%2Fv2%23%2Forg%2F%3Corganization%3E%2Fbilling%2Foverview>`__ 
      page displays.
