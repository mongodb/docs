.. _names:

=====
Names
=====

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. include:: /includes/styles/corrections.rst

Don't use real or copyrighted names in examples. For example, don't
use john.smith@example.com or sherlock.holmes@example.com.

Take care to include names that reflect the diversity of the real world.
Some example names include:

- Alex Martin
- Laura Garcia
- Jacob Meyer
- Marie Dubois
- Andre Robinson
- Riya Patel
- Wei Zhang

See the following topics for information about product names:

- :ref:`product-names-version-numbers`
- :ref:`third-party-names-and-trademarks`
