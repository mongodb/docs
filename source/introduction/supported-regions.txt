Cloud for Government Considerations
-----------------------------------

- You may deploy clusters in isolated, |fedramp|-compliant  
  regions. |aws| is the only cloud provider for |govcloud| regions.

- You cannot deploy clusters across government and commercial 
  regions.

Regions
-------

|govcloud| supports the following regions:

- ``us-gov-west-1`` (Northwestern US)

- ``us-gov-east-1`` (Northeastern US)
