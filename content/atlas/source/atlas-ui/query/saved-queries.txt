You can load saved queries and aggregations from the :guilabel:`My Queries` view 
once you are connected to your cluster. This page explains how to add and view 
favorite queries and aggregations.

Save an Aggregation Pipeline
----------------------------

You can save a pipeline for future use. If you load a saved pipeline, you can 
change it without changing the original saved copy. You can also create a 
:ref:`view <atlas-ui-view-details>` from your pipeline results.

To save your pipeline: 

.. include:: /atlas-ui/includes/shared/steps-save-pipeline.rst

.. _atlas-ui-favorite-queries:

Save a Favorite Query
---------------------

You can favorite a query for future use. If you load a favorite query, you can 
change it without changing the original saved copy.

To add a query to your favorites:

.. include:: /atlas-ui/includes/shared/steps-favorite-query.rst

.. _atlas-ui-view-favorite-queries:

View Saved Queries
------------------

You can view your saved queries and aggregation pipelines on the 
:guilabel:`My Queries` tab once connected to your cluster.

.. _atlas-ui-saved-query-view:

Use the :guilabel:`My Queries` Tab
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

When you click a saved or favorite query tile, |service| opens the
:guilabel:`Documents` tab with the filter loaded.

When you click a saved or favorite pipeline tile, |service| opens the 
:guilabel:`Aggregations` tab with the pipeline loaded.

.. _atlas-ui-saved-favorite-query-view:

Use the :guilabel:`Favorites` Tab
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

You can also view favorite queries from the :guilabel:`Favorites` tab 
from in the :guilabel:`Documents` view. To open the 
:guilabel:`Favorites` tab:
