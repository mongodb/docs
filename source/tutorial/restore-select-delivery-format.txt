====================================
Generate a Key Pair for SCP Restores
====================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

Overview
--------

When you restore a snapshot, you are given the option of accessing the
restore files through a link or of having |mms| transmit the restore files
via SCP to your server. To use SCP, you must generate a key pair |mms| can
use to transmit the restore.
