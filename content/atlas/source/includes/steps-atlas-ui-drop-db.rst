   .. step:: Drop the database.

      In the :guilabel:`Connections` sidebar, select or hover over the database 
      to drop and click on the trash can icon. A confirmation dialog appears. 

   .. step:: Confirm the database to delete. 

         Confirm by typing the name of the database, and click 
         :guilabel:`Drop Database`.
