   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-processes.rst
      
   .. step:: Select the :guilabel:`Clusters` view for your deployment.
      
   .. step:: Choose a cluster with a node you want to resync.

      Click the name of the deployment that contains the process to
      resync. |mms| opens the deployment :guilabel:`Overview`.
      
   .. step:: Choose a node to resync.

      a. Find the node that you want to resync.
      b. Click the :guilabel:`Actions` dropdown for that node.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Resync`.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Resync` to confirm.
      
   .. step:: At the top of the deployment :guilabel:`Overview`, click :guilabel:`Review & Deploy`

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Review & Deploy` to review your changes.
      
      |mms| displays your proposed changes.
      
      a. If you are satisfied, click :guilabel:`Confirm & Deploy`.
      b. If you want to make further configuration changes,
         click :guilabel:`Cancel`.
      
         Click :guilabel:`Modify` for the cluster to make additional changes.
