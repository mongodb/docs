About this Task
---------------

*Estimated completion time: 5 minutes*

Use this tutorial to load sample data into your |service|
{+database-deployments+}. You can load sample data into your |service|
{+database-deployment+} from the {+atlas-cli+} or the :ref:`{+atlas-ui+}
<atlas-ui>`. Select your interface from the drop-down menu. 

Before You Begin
----------------

To utilize the sample data provided by |service|, you must create
an |service| {+database-deployment+} to load data into. To learn more, 
see :ref:`create-new-database-deployment`.

To load sample data, you must also have 
:authrole:`Project Owner` access to the project.

.. include:: /includes/atlas-roles/org-owners-add-project.rst
