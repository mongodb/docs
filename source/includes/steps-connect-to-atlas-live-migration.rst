   .. step:: Connect to Atlas.
      
      In the :guilabel:`Organization Settings`, the :guilabel:`General Settings`
      tab displays the configurable settings for the organization.
      
      a. Click the :guilabel:`General Settings` tab.
      
      #. In the :guilabel:`Live Migration: Connect to Atlas` section,
         click :guilabel:`Connect to Atlas`. The :guilabel:`Connect to Atlas`
         dialog opens.
      
      #. Paste the link-token that you copied from |service-short|.
         The link-token contains details of your connection to |service-short|.
      
      #. Click :guilabel:`Connect to Atlas`.
