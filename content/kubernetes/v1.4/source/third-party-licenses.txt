.. meta::

.. default-domain:: mongodb

that may be distributed under licenses different than the MongoDB
software.

|k8s-op-short| depends upon the following third-party packages. These
packages are licensed as shown in the following list. Should MongoDB
have accidentally failed to list a required license, please
:website:`contact the MongoDB Legal Department </contact>`.
