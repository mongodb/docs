Overview
--------

You can update your password, email address, and mobile phone number. You can
also modify your |mms| display settings and newsletter preference. You
**cannot** change your username.

.. only:: cloud

   The username, email address, and password you use for |mms| are the
   same as you use for `<http://jira.mongodb.org>`_.
   Changing your email address or password in |mms| **will also
   change** those settings for jira.mongodb.org.

.. note::

   To change your two-factor authentication settings, see :doc:`/core/two-factor-authentication`.
