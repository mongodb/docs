=============
Organizations
=============

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

An organization can contain multiple projects (previously referred to
as groups). Under this hierarchy structure, you can:

- Use the same billing settings across all projects in your organization.

- Use the same alert settings across all projects in your organization.

- View all projects within an organization.

.. UNCOMMENT when teams rolls out

.. - Bulk assign users to projects.

.. _delete-organization:

Delete an Organization
----------------------

.. important::

   To delete an organization, you must have :authrole:`Organization
   Owner` role for the organization.

   You cannot delete an organization that has active projects. You must
   delete the organization's projects before you can delete the
   organization.
