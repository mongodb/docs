Overview
--------

You can update your password, email address, and mobile phone number. You can
also modify your |mms| display settings and newsletter preference. You
**cannot** change your username.

The username, email address, and password you use for |mms| are the same as you
use for `jira.mongodb.org <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_. Changing your email address
or password in |mms| **will also change** those settings for jira.mongodb.org.

.. note::

   To change your two-factor authentication settings, see :doc:`/core/two-factor-authentication`.

Change Password
---------------

Passwords must fulfill |mms|'s :ref:`password requirements
<password-requirements>`.

   Changing your password in |mms| will also change your password in
   `jira.mongodb.org <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_.
