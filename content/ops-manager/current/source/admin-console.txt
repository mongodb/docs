======================
Administration Console
======================

.. meta::
   :description: Access the administration console in Ops Manager if you have the Global Owner role.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

If you have the :authrole:`Global Owner` role for your |onprem|
deployment, click the :guilabel:`Admin` link in the top right corner of
the |application| to access the administration console.
