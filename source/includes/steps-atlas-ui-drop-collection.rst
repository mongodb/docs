.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Drop the collection.

      In the :guilabel:`Connections` sidebar, click the :icon-fa5:`ellipsis-h`
      icon next to the collection you want to drop. In the drop-down menu that 
      appears, click :guilabel:`Drop Collection`.

   .. step:: Confirm the collection to delete. 

         Confirm by typing the name of the collection, and click 
         :guilabel:`Drop Database`.
