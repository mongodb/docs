.. step:: Go to the :guilabel:`Private Endpoints for` |ear-key-provider| page. 

   In the :guilabel:`Advanced` page :guilabel:`Encryption at Rest
   using your Key Management` section, perform the following actions:

   a. Expand :guilabel:`Network Settings` for your |ear-key-provider|
      if it's collapsed. 
   b. Click :guilabel:`Manage`.
         
      The :guilabel:`Private Endpoints for` |ear-key-provider| page
      displays the regions, endpoint name, status of the |akv|
      private endpoints for your |service| {+clusters+}, and the
      actions you can take on the private endpoints.
