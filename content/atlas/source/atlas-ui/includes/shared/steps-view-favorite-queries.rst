.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Open query history

      Click the clock :icon-lg:`Clock` icon at the top of the 
      :guilabel:`Documents` tab.

   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Favorites` button in the past queries pane.

      Select your favorited operation.

      - If your favorited operation is a query, the :guilabel:`Favorites` list 
        view only displays a :guilabel:`Filter` statement, copy :icon-lg:`Copy` 
        icon, and delete :icon-lg:`Trash` icon.

        .. note::
            
            Clicking a favorited query in the 
            :guilabel:`Favorites` list view populates your 
            :guilabel:`Query Bar` with that query.

      - If your favorited operation is a bulk update statement, the 
        :guilabel:`Favorites` list view only displays a 
        :guilabel:`Filter` statement, :guilabel:`Update` statement, copy 
        :icon-lg:`Copy` icon, delete :icon-lg:`Trash` icon, and open new tab
        :icon-lg:`OpenNewTab` icon.

        .. note::

            If you click the open new tab :icon-lg:`OpenNewTab` icon, the 
            :guilabel:`Update Documents` modal opens with the bulk 
            update statement and the filter criteria. For details on bulk 
            update statements in |service|, see 
            :ref:`atlas-ui-bulk-update`.
