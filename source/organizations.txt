=============
Organizations
=============

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

An organization can contain multiple projects (previously referred to
as groups). Under this hierarchy structure, you can:

- Use the same billing settings across multiple projects in your organization.

- Use the same alert settings across multiple projects in your organization.

- View all projects within an organization.

.. - Bulk assign users to projects.

.. _create-organization:

Create an Organization
----------------------

When you create an organization, you are added as an Owner for the
organization.

Procedure
~~~~~~~~~

To create an organization:

#. In the upper-left-hand corner, click on your user name.

#. Click :guilabel:`Organizations`.

#. Click :guilabel:`New Organization`.

#. Enter the name for your organization.

#. Select |service| or |mms|.

#. Click :guilabel:`Next`.

#. Add members.

   a. For existing Atlas users, enter their username. Usually, this is
      the email the person used to register.

   b. For new Atlas users, enter their email address to send an invitation.

#. Specify the access for the members.

#. Click :guilabel:`Create Organization`.

.. _leave-organization:

Leave an Organization
---------------------

Prerequisites
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To leave an organization, another user must exist for the organization.

Procedure
~~~~~~~~~

To leave an organization:

#. In the upper-left-hand corner, click on your user name.

#. Click :guilabel:`Organizations`.

#. For the organization you wish to leave, click its :guilabel:`Leave` button.

#. In the :guilabel:`Leave Organization` dialog, click :guilabel:`Leave
   Organization`.
