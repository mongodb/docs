====================================================
Block MongoDB Support Access to Atlas Infrastructure
====================================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

As an organization owner, you can set up your |service| organization
so that MongoDB Production Support Employees, including Technical
Service Engineers, can only access your production servers with your
explicit permission. If a support issue arises and you want to grant
24-hour temporary infrastructure access to MongoDB Support, you can grant
access at the {+database-deployments+} level. 
You can also revoke temporary infrastructure access at any time
prior to the automatic 24-hour expiration.
