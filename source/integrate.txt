Overview
--------

The following related services can integrate with |service|:

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 30 70

   * - Product
     - Description

   * - `Triggers 
       <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/atlas/app-services/triggers/>`__
     - Execute application and database logic in |service|.

   * - `Device Sync
       <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/atlas/app-services/sync/>`__
     - Save data in the cloud, access data from other devices, and
       synchronize the data to the cloud to resolve any conflicting
       writes.

   * - `Managed Custom API Endpoints
       <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/atlas/app-services/data-api/custom-endpoints/>`__
     - Define custom |https| endpoints to create app-specific |api|
       routes or webhooks that integrate with external services.

   * - `MongoDB Charts <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/charts/>`__
     - Create visual representations of your MongoDB data and
       integrates seamlessly with |service|.

You can also use :ref:`the specific product integrations
<partner-integrations>` that we developed with our partners to enhance
|service| and partner service capabilities.
