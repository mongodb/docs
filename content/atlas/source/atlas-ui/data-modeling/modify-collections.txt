.. include:: /atlas-ui/includes/shared/data-modeling-modify.rst

In the :guilabel:`Data Modeling` tab, you can:

- Add or remove collections
- Rename collections

.. include:: /atlas-ui/includes/shared/fact-diagram-modification-data.rst

Procedure
---------

The following steps describe how you can add, edit, and remove collections 
in your |erd|:

.. procedure:: 
   :style: normal 

   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-data-explorer.rst

   .. include:: /atlas-ui/includes/shared/steps-data-modeling-open.rst

   .. step:: Add a new collection.

      To add a new collection, click the plus :icon-lg:`Plus` icon in the 
      upper-left corner of the diagram canvas. Clicking this icon opens a 
      drop-down menu with two options: 

      - :guilabel:`Add a new collection`
      - :guilabel:`Select from database`

      .. image:: /images/atlas-ui/compass/dm-add-collection.png
         :alt: Add collection icon in the Data Modeling tab 
         :width: 300px

      If you click :guilabel:`Select from database`, a dialog box opens that 
      allows you to select existing collections from your database that aren't 
      included in your diagram. 

      .. note:: 
         
         If you imported your diagram or you aren't connected to the database 
         used to generate your diagram, |service| prompts you to select a 
         database connection.

   .. include:: /atlas-ui/includes/shared/steps-collection-side-panel.rst

   .. step:: *(Optional)* Rename a collection.

       Under the :guilabel:`Name` section, specify a new name for your 
       collection.

   .. step:: *(Optional)* Delete a collection.

      In the side panel, click the trash :icon-lg:`Trash` icon.
