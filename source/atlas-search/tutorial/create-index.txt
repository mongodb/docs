Programatically
---------------

You can create an |fts| index programmatically by using
{+mongosh+} or a supported :driver:`MongoDB Driver </>` 
in your preferred language. 

Prerequisites
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

- ``M10`` or larger |service| clusters
- The following versions for each driver:

  .. list-table::
     :widths: 35 65
     :header-rows: 1
     :stub-columns: 1

     * - Driver
       - Version

     * - .NET/C# Driver
       - 2.21 or later

     * - Node.js Driver
       - 6.1 or later
