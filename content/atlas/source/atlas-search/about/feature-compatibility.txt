Other Limitations
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If you're using a :manual:`clustered collection </core/clustered-collections/>`
and have the :parameter:`notablescan <param.notablescan>` parameter set to
``true``, your |fts| indexes may not finish building. To resolve this issue, you
must set the ``notablescan`` parameter to ``false`` or check your log for index
status transitions.
