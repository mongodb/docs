.. procedure::
   :style: normal
      
   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-project-access-manager.rst

   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Users` tab.
      
   .. step:: Navigate to the appropriate tab for the resource you want to remove:
      
      - If it is not already displayed and you want to remove a user, click
        the :guilabel:`Users` tab.
      
      - If you want to remove a team, click the :guilabel:`Teams` tab.
      
   .. step:: Click :icon:`trash-alt` to the right of the user or team to remove.
      
   .. step:: Confirm the removal.
      
      - To confirm a user removal, click
        :guilabel:`Remove User from Organization`.
      
      - To confirm a team removal, click
        :guilabel:`Delete Team`."
