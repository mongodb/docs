.. note::

   When you upgrade the {+fcv+} to 9.0, MongoDB moves metadata from the
   config server to the shards. During this transition, the {+fcv+}
   upgrade drains and blocks chunk operations, including:

   - :dbcommand:`split`
   - :dbcommand:`mergeChunks`
   - :dbcommand:`mergeAllChunksOnShard`
   - Migrations, manual and balancer-issued

   Chunk operations resume after the {+fcv+} upgrade completes.
