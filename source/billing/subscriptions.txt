=========================
Subscriptions and Billing
=========================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You must purchase a subscription to use |cloudgov|. Elastic invoicing 
is not available.

Purchase a Subscription
-----------------------

You can purchase a |cloudgov| subscription from the following 
sources:

- The AWS marketplace

- :website:`MongoDB Sales </contact/atlas>`

Activate Your Subscription
--------------------------

Activate your subscription using the activation code you receive after 
purchase.

To learn more about |cloudgov-short| subscriptions, see 
:ref:`billing`.
