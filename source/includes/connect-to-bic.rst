To connect to the |bic|:

.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Connect` 
      button for your cluster.

   .. step:: Select :guilabel:`Standard Connection` and 
      click :guilabel:`Choose a connection method`.

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Connect Your Business Intelligence Tool` 
      and use the provided connection information to connect with 
      your BI tool.

For more information on connecting to the |bic|, see
:ref:`Connection Tutorials <bic-connection-tutorials>`.
