====================
Alert Email History
====================

.. meta::
   :description: View and filter the list of alert emails sent by Ops Manager and their recipients to audit notification delivery or troubleshoot missing or delayed alerts.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

This page in the |onprem| UI displays alert emails that |onprem| has sent and
their recipients. Use this page to audit notification delivery
or troubleshoot missing alerts. To filter the list, type an
email address into the
:guilabel:`Search emails by recipient` :icon-fa4:`search` box.
