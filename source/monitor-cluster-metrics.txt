=========================
Monitor |service| Cluster
=========================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

|service| collects metrics for your cluster and provides charts for
analyzing the data collected. |service| warns you if disk use, IOPS, or
database operations exceed certain thresholds.

To view metrics, from :guilabel:`Clusters` view, select the
:guilabel:`Clusters` tab and click :guilabel:`Metrics`.
