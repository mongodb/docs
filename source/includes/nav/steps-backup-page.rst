.. step:: Go to the :guilabel:`Backup` page for your {+cluster+}.

   a. Click your {+cluster+}\'s name.

   #. Click the :guilabel:`Backup` tab.
   
      If the cluster has no :guilabel:`Backup` tab, then |service|
      backups are disabled for that {+database-deployment+} and no 
      snapshots are available. You can enable backups when you
      :ref:`scale the cluster <scale-cluster-backups>`.

      The `Backup <https://cloud.mongodb.com/go?l=https%3A%2F%2Fcloud.mongodb.com%2Fv2%2F%3Cproject%3E%23%2Fclusters%2Fbackup%2F%3Ccluster%3E>`__ page displays.
