==========================================
Administration Console : Control Panel Tab
==========================================

.. meta::

The :guilabel:`Control Panel` tab in the |onprem|
Administration Console provides tools for managing alert
notifications. Review the history of email and |sms| messages
that |onprem| sends. Send test messages to verify that your
notification channels are configured.
