=========================
atlas clusters sampleData
=========================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1

Manage sample data for your cluster.

The sampleData command provides access to sample data to be loaded onto your cluster.
