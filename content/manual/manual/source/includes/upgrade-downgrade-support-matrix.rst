- For any versions, major or minor, you can upgrade or downgrade to its
  adjacent neighbors. For example, you can downgrade from 8.3
  to 8.2 or upgrade from 7.0 to 8.0.
- For all versions, major or minor, you can downgrade to the
  immediately previous major version. For example, from
  8.3 to 8.0.
- For any minor version, you can upgrade to the immediately next
  version. For example, from 8.2 to 8.3.
