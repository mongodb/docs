.. setting:: Weekly Retention of Snapshots (in Weeks)

   *Type*: integer

   *Default*: 2

   
   Specifies how many weeks a weekly snapshot is stored.
   
   Accepted values are:
   
   
   - **No weekly retention**
   - One of the following integers: **1**, **2**, **3**, **4**, **5**,
     **6**, **7**, **8**, **12**, **16**, **20**, **24**, and **52**
   
   
   Corresponds to :setting:`brs.snapshotSchedule.retention.weekly`.
