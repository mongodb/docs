Considerations
---------------

- The support lifecycle of the |k8s-op|
  is one year from the release date of a major version.

- Versioning of the |k8s-op-short| follows the semantic versioning convention.

- The End of Life dates in this section might be subject to change.

   :widths: 50 50

     - End of Life Date
