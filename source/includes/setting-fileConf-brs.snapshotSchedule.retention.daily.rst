.. setting:: brs.snapshotSchedule.retention.daily

   *Type*: integer

   *Default*: 0

   
   Specifies how many days a daily snapshot is stored.
   
   Accepted values are:
   
   ``0``, ``3``, ``4``, ``5``, ``6``, ``7``, ``15``, ``30``, ``60``,
   ``90``, ``120``, ``180`` or ``360``
   
   
   Corresponds to ``.Daily Retention of Snapshots``
