Edit Your User Account
----------------------

.. cond:: onprem

   To access your profile settings, click on your user name in the
   upper-right hand corner and select :guilabel:`Account`. Click the
   :guilabel:`Profile` tab.

   To edit a setting, click its pencil icon:

   .. list-table::
      :widths: 30 70
      :stub-columns: 1

      * - User Name

        - Displays your username. You cannot change your username.

      * - Email Address

        - Displays the email address |mms| associates with your
          account.

      * - Mobile Phone Number

        - The number to use to receive SMS alerts, including two-factor
          authentication codes.

      * - Password

        - Allows you to change your |mms| password.

          Passwords must be at least 8 characters long and contain at
          least one letter, one digit, and one special character.

      * - Two-Factor Authentication

        - |mms| may require two factor authentication for login. To
          learn more, see :doc:`/core/two-factor-authentication`.

          To delete or reset two-factor authentication, contact your
          |mms| system administrator.

.. cond:: cloud

   To access your profile and contact settings, click on your name in
   the upper-right hand corner and select :guilabel:`MongoDB Account`.
   Click :guilabel:`Personal Info` from the left navigation menu.

   .. important::

      The following sites also use your user name, email address, and password:

      - `jira.mongodb.org <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_
      - :mdb-sup:`MongoDB Support </welcome>`
      - :mdbu:`MongoDB University </>`

      Changing your password in |mms| also changes the password you
      need to log into these services.

   .. list-table::
      :widths: 30 70
      :stub-columns: 1

      * - First Name and Last Name

        - Your first and last name. You cannot change your name.

      * - Company

        - The name of your company.

      * - Position

        - Your position in your company.

      * - Country

        - The country |mms| associates with your account.

      * - Email Address

        - The email address |mms| associates with your account.
          You cannot change your email address.

      * - Phone Number

        - The number to use to receive SMS alerts, including two-factor
          authentication codes.

Edit Your Personal Preferences
------------------------------

.. cond:: onprem

   To access your preference settings, click on your user name in the
   upper-right hand corner and select :guilabel:`Account`. Click the
   :guilabel:`Personalization` tab.

.. cond:: cloud

   To access your preference settings, click on :guilabel:`Preferences`
   at the top of the page. Click :guilabel:`Personalization` in the
   left navigation pane.

   The available fields to edit depend on your
   :doc:`role </reference/user-roles>`.

View/Edit Organization Membership
---------------------------------
.. cond:: onprem

   To view your organizations, click on your user name in the upper-right
   hand corner and select :guilabel:`Organizations`.

.. cond:: cloud

   To view your organization, click on :guilabel:`Preferences` at the
   top of the page and click :guilabel:`Organizations` in the left
   navigation pane.

The page displays the |mms| organizations to which you belong.

- To add a new organization, click :guilabel:`New Organization`. To
  learn more, see :ref:`create-organization`.

- To leave an organization, click the :guilabel:`Leave` button for that
  organization.
