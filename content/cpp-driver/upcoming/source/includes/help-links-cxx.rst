- Ask for help in the MongoDB `Stack Overflow page <https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/mongodb%20c%2b%2b>`__
  or in the MongoDB `Reddit community <https://www.reddit.com/r/mongodb/>`__.
- Visit our :technical-support:`Support Channels </>`.
- See how to :ref:`report a bug <cpp-issues-help>`.
