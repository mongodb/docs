   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Suspend Automation` to confirm.
      
   .. step:: At the top of the deployment :guilabel:`Overview`, click :guilabel:`Review & Deploy`
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Confirm and Deploy`.
