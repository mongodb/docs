   .. step:: Navigate to your |bic-short| instance.
      
      a. Click the cluster card that has the |bic-short| instance.
      
      #. In the :guilabel:`View` field in upper-left corner, click 
         the :guilabel:`BI Connector` option.
      
   .. step:: Modify the |bic-short| instance.
      
      a. Click :guilabel:`Modify`.
      
      #. Update any settings that you need to change.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Apply Changes`.

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Review & Deploy` to review your changes.
      
      |mms| displays your proposed changes.
      
      a. If you are satisfied, click :guilabel:`Confirm & Deploy`.
      b. If you want to make further configuration changes, 
         click :guilabel:`Cancel`. Click :guilabel:`Modify` for the
         cluster to make additional changes.
