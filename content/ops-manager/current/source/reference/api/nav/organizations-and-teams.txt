=======================
Organizations and Teams
=======================

.. meta::
   :description: Explore the Ops Manager API resources for managing organizations and teams, including endpoints for creating, updating, and deleting organizations and team members.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

Organizations group related projects and users in |onprem|.
Teams allow you to manage user access to projects more
efficiently by assigning roles to a group rather than to
individual users. The |mms| :doc:`API </core/api>` exposes
the following organizations and teams resources:

- :doc:`/reference/api/organizations`
- :doc:`/reference/api/teams`
