Delete an App

If you have more than one App, you will be prompted to select one or
multiple apps that you would like to delete from a list of all your Atlas App Services Apps.
The list includes App Services Apps from all projects associated with your user profile.

Syntax
------

.. code-block::

   realm-cli apps delete [options]

Options
-------

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 20 10 10 60

   * - Name
     - Type
     - Required
     - Description
   * - -a, --app
     - strings
     - false
     - Specify the name(s) or ID(s) of App Services Apps to delete
   * - -h, --help
     - 
     - false
     - help for delete
