Overview
--------

|onprem| can pass all outgoing requests through an 
:abbr:`HTTP (HyperText Transfer Protocol)` or 
:abbr:`HTTPS (Secure HyperText Transfer Protocol)` proxy.
This lets |onprem| deployments without direct access to external
resources access outside notification services and the MongoDB
:term:`version manifest`.
