Choropleth Licenses
-------------------

The following lists the third-party TopoJSON shape schemes used in
|charts-short| for choropleths.

.. list-table::
   :widths: 20 40
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Country
     - Description

   * - `Australia
       <http://www.abs.gov.au/websitedbs/D3310114.nsf/Home/Creative+Commons+licensing>`__
     - Australian map shape files based on `Australian Bureau of
       Statistics data <http://www.abs.gov.au/websitedbs/D3310114.nsf/home/Digital+Boundaries>`__, 
       licensed under the
       `Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International <https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/>`__
       license. We have transformed the source data into TopoJSON format.

   * - `United Kingdom
       <http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/>`__
     - UK map shape files contain public sector information licensed
       under the `Open Government Licence v3.0 <http://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/>`__.
   * - `Canada <https://www.statcan.gc.ca/eng/reference/licence>`__
     - Canadian map shape files adapted from Statistics Canada, 2011
       Census Boundary files, referenced on February 21, 2019. This
       does not constitute an endorsement by Statistics Canada of this
       product.

   * - `US, Germany, World <https://www.npmjs.com/package/topojson-client/v/3.1.0>`__
     - Copyright 2012-2019 Michael Bostock Copyright 2019 Mike Bostock
