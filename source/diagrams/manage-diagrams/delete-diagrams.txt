.. _rm-delete-diagrams:

================
Delete a Diagram
================

.. meta::
   :description: Delete diagrams in Relational Migrator by navigating to the diagram, opening the Diagram Properties pane, and confirming the deletion.

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can delete diagrams on the :guilabel:`Mapping` screen.

About this Task 
---------------

Relational Migrator requires at least one diagram in your project. You
must have at least two diagrams before you can delete a diagram.

Steps 
-----

.. procedure::
   :style:  connected

   .. step:: Navigate to the diagram you want to delete

      Click the name of the diagram in the Diagram Toolbar to display the
      diagram. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram-toolbar.png
         :alt: Diagram toolbar   

   .. step:: Open the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane

      Click the :icon-fa5:`chevron-circle-right` arrow button to open
      the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane. 

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram-properties.png
         :alt: Diagram Properties pane

      The :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane displays the active diagram name and
      notes. 

   .. step:: Click the :icon-fa5:`ellipsis-h` button in the :guilabel:`Diagram Properties` pane

      A menu opens.

      .. image:: /img/diagrams/diagram-options.png
         :alt: Diagram Properties Options menu

   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Delete Diagram` option

      A confirmation box opens.

   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Delete Diagram` button in the confirmation box. 

      Once you confirm that you want to delete the selected diagram,
      Relational Migrator deletes the diagram. This step cannot be
      undone.
