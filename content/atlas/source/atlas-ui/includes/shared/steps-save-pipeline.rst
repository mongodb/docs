.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Click the save dropdown button.

      In the :guilabel:`Aggregations` pane, open the :guilabel:`Save` drop-down 
      menu and click ``Save as``.

      .. figure:: /images/atlas-ui/compass/query-save-pipeline.png
         :alt: Save pipeline as
         :width: 600px

   .. step:: Enter a name for your pipeline.

   .. step:: Save the pipeline.

      Click the :guilabel:`Save` button to save your pipeline. Your pipeline 
      will be saved under the folder :icon-lg:`Folder` icon in the top-left 
      corner of the pipeline builder.
