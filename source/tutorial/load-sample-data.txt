======================
Prepare Your S3 Bucket
======================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

*Estimated completion time: 10 minutes*

Before creating your first {+data-lake-short+}, you must first
create and load an S3 bucket with sample data. This part of
the tutorial walks you through preparing your S3 bucket.

Prerequisites
-------------

To complete this part of the tutorial, you will need an
`AWS account <https://aws.amazon.com/premiumsupport/knowledge-center/create-and-activate-aws-account/>`__.
