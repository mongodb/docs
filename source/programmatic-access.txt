Introduction to Programmatic Usage
----------------------------------

AtlasGov supports programmatic access using the following resources. However, 
AtlasGov is designed with additional security, compliance, and operational 
considerations to meet the needs of organizations that handle sensitive data. To
learn more about these limitations, see `Atlas for Government Considerations <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/atlas/government/api/#atlas-for-government-considerations>`_.

AtlasGov supports the following DevOps tools: 

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 30 70

   * - Product
     - Description

   * - `Atlas Kubernetes Operator <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/atlas/atlas-operator/>`__
     - Manage |service| resources alongside your |k8s|-based applications, 
       through your existing |k8s| workflow.

   * - `HashiCorp Terraform MongoDB Atlas Provider 
       <https://registry.terraform.io/providers/mongodb/mongodbatlas/latest/docs#configure-mongodb-atlas-for-government>`__
     - Integrate |service| into your continuous delivery workflows
       with the official plugin that is verified and tested by
       HashiCorp. To learn how to get started, see
       :ref:`getting-started-terraform`.
