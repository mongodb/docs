.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-processes.rst
   
   .. step:: Select the :guilabel:`Clusters` view for your deployment.
      
   .. step:: On the line for any process, click the ellipsis [:guilabel:`...`] icon then click :guilabel:`Monitoring Settings`.
      
   .. step:: Toggle :guilabel:`Collect Logs For Host` as desired.
      
      a. Click the :guilabel:`Logs` tab.
      b. Toggle the :guilabel:`Collect Logs For Host` to :guilabel:`Off` or
         :guilabel:`On`, as desired.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`X` to close the :guilabel:`Monitoring Settings` box.
      
      If you turn off log collection, existing log entries remain in the
      :guilabel:`Logs` tab, but |mms| does not collect new entries.
