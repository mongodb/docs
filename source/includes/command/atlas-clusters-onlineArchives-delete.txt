====================================
atlas clusters onlineArchives delete
====================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page

Delete an online archive from a cluster.

Syntax
------

.. code-block::

   atlas clusters onlineArchives delete <archiveId> [options]

---------
