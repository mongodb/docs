.. procedure::
   :style: normal
      
   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-online-archive.rst
      
   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Connect` button for the Online Archive to which you want to connect.
      
   .. step:: Choose your connection method.

      You can connect through a :ref:`Driver <connect-via-driver>`, 
      :ref:`Compass <atlas-connect-via-compass>`, and the :ref:`MongoDB Shell 
      <connect-mongo-shell>`.
      
   .. step:: Select :guilabel:`Connect to Online Archive` to get the connection string for connecting to your Online Archive.

      You can use this connection string to run read-only, federated 
      queries against your ``Online Archive``.
