{+azure+} Data Transfer Costs for {+atlas-sp+}
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Current Pricing (Until December 3, 2025)
````````````````````````````````````````

{+atlas-sp+} data transfer costs for {+azure+} currently depend on the
destination region.

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :stub-columns: 1
   :widths: 25 15

   * - Destination {+azure+} Region
     - Price per GB

   * - ``eastus`` Virginia, USA
     - $0.088

   * - ``eastus2`` Virginia, USA
     - $0.088

   * - ``westus`` California, USA
     - $0.088

   * - ``westeurope`` Netherlands
     - $0.088

New Pricing (Effective December 3, 2025)
````````````````````````````````````````

Beginning December 3rd, 2025, {+atlas-sp+} data transfer costs depend
on the relationship between the origin region and the destination.

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :stub-columns: 1
   :widths: 40 20

   * - Data Transfer Type
     - Price per GB

   * - Same Region
     - No charge

   * - Same Continent
     - $0.02 to $0.16

   * - Different Continent
     - $0.05 to $0.16

   * - Out to Internet
     - $0.087 to $0.181
