.. _sample-data:

==============================
Sample Data for Atlas Clusters
==============================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

|service| provides sample datasets you can load in to your |service|
clusters. You can use these datasets to quickly get started
experimenting with data interactivity tools in |service|, such as the
:ref:`data-explorer`.
