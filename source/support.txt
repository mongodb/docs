=======
Support
=======

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

The same support guarantees that apply to |service| apply to 
|cloudgov|, including a 99.9999% uptime Service Level Agreement (SLA).

To learn more about commercial |service| support, see 
:atlas:`Support </support/>`.

Additional support guarantees depend on the region to which your 
resources are deployed.

Deployments in |aws-fr-moderate|
--------------------------------

No additional support guarantees.

Deployments in |aws-fr-high| 
----------------------------

You are guaranteed support from a U.S. citizen operating on U.S. soil, 
per |fedramp| high impact level requirements.
