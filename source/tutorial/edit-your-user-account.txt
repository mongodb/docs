Edit Your User Account
----------------------

.. cond:: onprem

   To access your profile settings, click on your user name in the
   upper-right hand corner and select :guilabel:`Account`. Click the
   :guilabel:`Profile` tab.

   To edit a setting, click its pencil icon:

   .. list-table::
      :widths: 30 70

      * - :guilabel:`User Name`

        - Displays your username. You cannot change your username.

      * - :guilabel:`Email Address`

        - Displays the email address |mms| associates with your account.

      * - :guilabel:`Mobile Phone Number`

        - The number to use to receive SMS alerts, including two-factor
          authentication codes.

      * - :guilabel:`Password`

        - Allows you to change your |mms| password. 

          Passwords must be at least 8 characters long and contain at
          least one letter, one digit, and one special character.

          Although the password requirements are different for `MongoDB
          Jira account <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_ and |mms|, passwords
          for `MongoDB Jira <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_ and |mms| are
          the same, and changing your password in |mms| will also
          change your password for JIRA.

      * - :guilabel:`Two-Factor Authentication`

        - |mms| may require two factor authentication for login. For details, see
          :doc:`/core/two-factor-authentication`.

          To delete or reset two-factor authentication, contact your |mms|
          system administrator.

.. cond:: cloud

   To access your profile and contact settings, click on your name in the 
   upper-right hand corner and select :guilabel:`MongoDB Account`. 
   Click :guilabel:`Personal Info` from the left navigation menu.

   .. important::

      Your username, email address, and password are also used
      for `jira.mongodb.org <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_, 
      `MongoDB Support <https://support.mongodb.com/welcome>`_, and 
      `MongoDB University <https://university.mongodb.com/>`_. Changing 
      your password in |mms| will also change the password you use to log 
      into these services.

   .. list-table::
      :widths: 30 70

      * - :guilabel:`First Name` and :guilabel:`Last Name`

        - Your first and last name. You cannot change your name.

      * - :guilabel:`Company`

        - The name of your company. 

      * - :guilabel:`Position`

        - Your position in the company.

      * - :guilabel:`Country`

        - The country |mms| associates with your account.

      * - :guilabel:`Email Address`

        - The email address |mms| associates with your account. 
          You cannot change your email address.

      * - :guilabel:`Phone Number`

        - The number to use to receive SMS alerts, including two-factor
          authentication codes.

Edit Your Personal Preferences
------------------------------

.. cond:: onprem 

   To access your preference settings, click on your user name in the
   upper-right hand corner and select :guilabel:`Account`. Click the
   :guilabel:`Personalization` tab.

.. only:: cloud
   
   To access your preference settings, click on :guilabel:`Preferences` 
   at the top of the page. Click the :guilabel:`Personalization` tab.
   
   The available fields to edit depend on your :doc:`role
   </reference/user-roles>`.

View/Edit Organization Membership
---------------------------------
.. cond:: onprem

   To view your organizations, click on your user name in the upper-right
   hand corner and select :guilabel:`Organizations`.

.. cond:: cloud

   To view your organization, click on :guilabel:`Preferences` at the 
   top of the page and click the :guilabel:`Organizations` tab.

The page displays the |mms| organizations to which you belong.

To add a new organization, click :guilabel:`New Organization`. For more
information, see :ref:`create-organization`.

To leave an organization, click the :guilabel:`Leave` button for that
organization.
