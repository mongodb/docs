   :style: normal

   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-project-alerts.rst
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Alert Settings`.
      
   .. step:: Choose the alert setting to delete.
      
      a. On the row for a specific alert, click :icon:`ellipsis-h`
         then :guilabel:`Delete`.
      
         The :guilabel:`Delete` option is disabled if you don't have 
         one of the following roles:
      
         - :authrole:`Project Owner`
         - :authrole:`Organization Owner` 
      
      #. Click :guilabel:`Confirm` in the :guilabel:`Confirm Delete` modal
         or click :guilabel:`Cancel` to leave the alert as configured.
