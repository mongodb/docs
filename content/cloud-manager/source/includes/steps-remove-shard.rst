.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-processes.rst
   
   .. step:: Select the :guilabel:`Clusters` view for your deployment.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Modify` on the cluster where you want to remove a shard.
      
   .. step:: In the :guilabel:`Member Configuration` section, remove a shard.
      
      a. Expand :guilabel:`SHARD SETTINGS`.
      
      b. Click the :icon-mms:`ellipsis` next to the shard you want to remove.

      c. Click :guilabel:`Remove from Cluster`.
