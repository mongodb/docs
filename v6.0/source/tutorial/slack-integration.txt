====================
Integrate with Slack
====================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. meta::
   :keywords: slack, integration, add slack, integrate with slack

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can integrate |mms| with  `Slack <https://slack.com/>`__ to receive
|mms| alerts in your Slack channel.

Prerequisites
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To integrate |mms| with Slack, you must:

- Have a `Slack workspace <https://slack.com/help/articles/206845317-Create-a-Slack-workspace/>`__.

- Have `sufficient permissions to install a Slack app in your workspace <https://slack.com/help/articles/222386767-Manage-app-approval-for-your-workspace/>`__.
