.. tip:: {+cluster-ai-assist+}
   
   MongoDB offers an {+cluster-ai-assist+} you can use while creating or
   editing a cluster to answer natural-language questions about cluster
   configuration. The {+cluster-ai-assist+} can recommend settings based
   on your workload and needs and apply those settings directly to your cluster.

   To learn more about the {+cluster-ai-assist+}, see 
   :ref:`AI Cluster Assistant <atlas-ai-cluster-assistant>`.
