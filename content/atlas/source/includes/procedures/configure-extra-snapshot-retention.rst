.. procedure::
   :style: normal
	   
   .. step:: In the :guilabel:`Extra Snapshot Retention` section,
      select the frequency unit from the :guilabel:`Frequency Unit`
      dropdown for an existing policy item.

      Alternatively, click :guilabel:`Add Another Region and
      Frequency` to add extra snapshot retention for another region.

   .. step:: Select the :guilabel:`Retention Time` as a combination of
      number and interval.

   .. step:: Toggle :guilabel:`Point in Time Restore` according to
      whether or not you want to enable :ref:`{+PIT-Restore+}
      <create-pit-policy>` for snapshot copies in that region.

   .. step:: At the bottom of the page, click :guilabel:`Review
      Changes` to see a summary of differences between your existing
      backup policy and your new backup policy.

   .. step:: Click
      :guilabel:`Confirm`.
