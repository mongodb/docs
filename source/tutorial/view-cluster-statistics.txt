==================================
View Aggregated Cluster Statistics
==================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

Overview
--------

Cluster statistics let you view data for the whole cluster at once. You
can compare components dynamically across the cluster and can view both
individual and aggregated data. You can pinpoint moments in time and
isolate data to specific components.
