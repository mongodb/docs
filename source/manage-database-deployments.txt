View All {+Database-Deployments+}
---------------------------------

The :guilabel:`{+Database-Deployments+}` view displays all {+clusters+} 
and {+serverless-instances+} to which the user belongs.

.. _view-cluster-details:

View {+Database-Deployment+} Details
------------------------------------

Through the :guilabel:`{+Database-Deployment+} Detail` view, |service| 
displays {+clusters+} and {+serverless-instances+} grouped into their 
:ref:`Project <projects>`.

.. include:: /includes/steps/view-clusters-in-project.rst

Take the Next Steps
-------------------

After you view your {+database-deployments+}, you can:

.. list-table::
   :widths: 40 60
   :header-rows: 1

   * - Action
     - Description

   * - :doc:`/manage-clusters`
     - Manage your |service| {+clusters+} with {+cluster+}-specific 
       options.
   
   * - :doc:`/manage-serverless-instances`
     - Manage your |service| {+serverless-instances+} with 
       serverless-specific options.

   * - :ref:`global-clusters`
     - Manage your |service| |global-write-clusters|. |service| 
       |global-write-clusters| require that you define single or
       multi-region :guilabel:`Zones`. You can also
       :ref:`shard global collections for global writes
       <de-shard-collection-for-global-writes>`.
