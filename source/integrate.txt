Overview
--------

The following products can integrate with |service|:

.. list-table::
   :header-rows: 1
   :widths: 30 70

   * - Product
     - Description

   * - :ref:`Atlas Kubernetes Operator <atlas-operator-top-ref>`
     - Lets you manage |service| resources without leaving |k8s|.

   * - `{+MongoDB-Realm+} <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/realm/>`__
     - Provides a managed cloud environment backed by |service|
       for you to write and host a full application.

   * - `MongoDB Charts <https://www.mongodb.com/docs/charts/saas/>`__
     - Creates visual representations of your MongoDB data and
       integrates seamlessly with |service|.

You can also use :ref:`the specific product integrations
<partner-integrations>` that we developed with our partners to enhance
|service| and partner service capabilities.
