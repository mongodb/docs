=============================================
mongocli iam organizations invitations delete
=============================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1

Syntax
------

.. code-block::

   mongocli iam organizations invitations delete <invitationId> [options]
