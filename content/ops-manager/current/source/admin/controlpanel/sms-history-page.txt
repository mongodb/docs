===================
Alert |sms| History
===================

.. meta::
   :description: View and filter the history of alert SMS messages sent by Ops Manager and their recipients to audit notification delivery or troubleshoot missing alerts.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

This page in the |onprem| UI lists the alert |sms| messages that |onprem| sends
and their recipients. Use this page to audit |sms|
notification delivery or troubleshoot missing alerts. To
filter the list, enter a telephone number into the
:guilabel:`Search SMSs by recipient` :icon-fa4:`search`
box.
