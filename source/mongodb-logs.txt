==============================
View and Download MongoDB Logs
==============================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

Each :binary:`mongod <bin.mongod>`, :binary:`mongos <bin.mongos>`, and
:bic:`mongosqld </reference/mongosqld>` 
instance keeps an account of its activity in its own log file. |service|
retains the last 30 days of :manual:`log messages
</reference/log-messages>` and :manual:`system event audit messages
</reference/audit-message>`. The Performance
Advisor retains at most 7 days of logs.

.. important::

   You must have :authrole:`Project Data Access Read Only` privileges 
   or greater to download logs.

.. note::

   ``M0`` {+free-clusters+} and ``M2/M5`` {+shared-clusters+} do not provide downloadable logs.
