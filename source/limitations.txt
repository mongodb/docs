======================
{+dl+} Limitations
======================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. meta::
   :keywords: {+dl+}

- {+adl+} doesn't support ``M0``, ``M2``, or ``M5`` clusters. It 
  supports ``M10`` or higher clusters only.
- {+adl+} doesn't support sharded clusters.
