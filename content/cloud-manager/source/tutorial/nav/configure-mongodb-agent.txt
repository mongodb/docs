==============================================
Configure the {+mdbagent+} for Access Control
==============================================

.. meta::
   :description: Configure the MongoDB Agent for access control by ensuring it authenticates with the necessary roles and privileges for SCRAM, LDAP, Kerberos, or X.509 in your MongoDB deployment.
   :robots: noindex, nosnippet 

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

If your MongoDB deployment enforces access control, the {+mdbagent+}
must authenticate to your MongoDB databases as a user with the proper :manual:`roles </reference/built-in-roles>` and
:manual:`privileges </reference/privilege-actions>`.

To configure the {+mdbagent+} for your deployment, select your
authentication mechanism:

- :ref:`SCRAM-SHA <configure-agent-for-scram>`
- :ref:`LDAP <configure-agent-for-ldap>`
- :ref:`Kerberos <configure-agent-for-kerberos>`
- :ref:`X.509 <configure-agent-for-x509>`
