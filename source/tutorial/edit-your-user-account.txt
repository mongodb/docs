Edit Your User Account
----------------------

Click :guilabel:`Settings` and then click :guilabel:`Account`. To edit a
setting click the its pencil icon:

.. only:: cloud

   .. important::

      Your username, email address, and password are also used
      for `jira.mongodb.org <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_. Changing your
      email address or password in |mms| will also change the email
      address and password you use to log into `jira.mongodb.org
      <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_.

.. list-table::
   :widths: 30 70

   * - :guilabel:`User Name`

     - Displays your username. You cannot change your username.

   * - :guilabel:`Email Address`

     - Displays the email address |mms| associates with your account.

       .. only:: cloud

          Changing your email address in |mms| will also change your
          email address in `jira.mongodb.org
          <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_.

   * - :guilabel:`Mobile Phone Number`

     - The number to use to receive SMS alerts, including two-factor
       authentication codes.

   * - :guilabel:`Password`

     - Allows you to change your |mms| password. Passwords must be at
       least 8 characters long and contain at least one letter, one
       digit, and one special character.

       .. only:: cloud

          Although the password requirements are different for `MongoDB
          Jira account <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_ and |mms|, passwords
          for `MongoDB Jira <http://jira.mongodb.org>`_ and |mms| are
          the same, and changing your password in |mms| will also
          change your password for JIRA.

   * - :guilabel:`Two-Factor Authentication`

     - |mms| may require two factor authentication for login. For details, see
       :doc:`/core/two-factor-authentication`.

       .. only:: cloud

          To delete or reset two-factor authentication, `contact Support
          <https://cloud.mongodb.com/links/support>`_.

       .. only:: onprem

          To delete or reset two-factor authentication, contact your |mms|
          system administrator.

Edit Group Membership
---------------------

Click :guilabel:`Settings` and then click :guilabel:`My Groups`. The page
displays the |mms| groups to which you belong.

To add a new group, click :guilabel:`Add Group`.

To leave a group, click the group's :guilabel:`Leave Group` button.
