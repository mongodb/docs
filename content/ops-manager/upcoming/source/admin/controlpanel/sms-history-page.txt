===========
SMS History
===========

.. meta::
   :description: View and filter the history of alert SMS messages sent to recipients by entering a phone number.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

This page displays alert |sms| messages sent and their recipients. To
filter the list, type a telephone number (digits only) into the
:guilabel:`Search SMSs by recipient` :icon-fa4:`search` box.
