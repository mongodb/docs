================================
Remove Processes from Monitoring
================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

Overview
--------

You can remove processes that you no longer use.

Instead of removing a process, you can optionally disable alerts for the
process, which does not remove it from the :guilabel:`Deployment` pages. See
:doc:`/tutorial/manage-host-alerts`.
