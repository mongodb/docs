   .. step:: Choose the alert setting to delete.
      
      a. On the row for a specific alert, click :icon:`ellipsis-h`
         then :guilabel:`Delete`.
      #. Click :guilabel:`Delete` in the :guilabel:`Delete Alert` modal or
         click :guilabel:`Cancel` to leave the alert as configured.
      
         .. note::
      
            If you have not logged in within the five minutes of pushing
            :guilabel:`Delete`, you need to re-enter your password, then
            click :guilabel:`Delete` again.
