.. _embedding-charts:

====================================
Embed Charts in Your Web Application
====================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

As a dashboard :ref:`Author <dashboard-permissions>`, you allow your
charts to be embedded in external websites or shared via links. You can
either:

- Allow anyone with the chart's identifying information to share it or
  embed it in a web page, or
  
- Require users to authenticate through an
  :guilabel:`Embedding Authentication Provider` to share and embed the
  chart.

.. _choose-access-level:

How to Choose an Access Level
-----------------------------

If you want strict control over who can access your charts, use the
:guilabel:`Authenticed` embedding option. If your data
is non-sensitive and you prefer a simpler embedding solution, use the
:guilabel:`Unauthenticated` option.

.. note::
   
   :atlas:`Project Owners </reference/user-roles#project-roles>` can
   configure Authentication Providers from the
   :ref:`Admin Settings <admin-settings>` page.
