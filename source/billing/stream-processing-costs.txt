Stream Processing
=================

.. important::

   {+atlas-sp+} is currently in Public Preview.

   For information on limitations to {+atlas-sp+} Public Preview
   functionality, see :ref:`atlas-sp-limitations`.

You incur {+atlas-sp+} costs for the following items: 

- Stream Processing Instance (SPI)
- Data Transfer

.. note:: Limited Cloud Provider and Regional Availability

   As of Public Preview, {+atlas-sp+} is only available in the 
   |AWS| ``us-east-1`` region.

Stream Processing Instance (SPI)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

|service| charges per hour per {+SPI+} worker, where the worker resources and the 
hourly rate are determined by the {+SPI+} tier. You are billed when there is 
at least one stream processor running in your {+SPI+} tier.

For Public Preview, |service| charges a promotional price of $0.21 per hour (with GA price 
of $0.39 per hour) for an ``SP30`` worker, and each worker provides 2 vCPU and 8 GB of RAM.

.. note:: Exceptions to Stream Processing Instance Cost

   As of Public Preview, |service| doesn't charge for storage. The pricing currently 
   described here is promotional pricing for Public Preview.

.. note:: Upcoming {+SPI+} Tiers
   
   Upcoming {+SPI+} tiers for {+atlas-sp+} include the ``SP10`` worker tier, which 
   provides 1 vCPU and 2 GB of RAM at the price of $0.19 per hour, and the ``SP50`` 
   worker tier, which provides 8 vCPU and 32 GB of RAM at the price of $1.56 per 
   hour.

Data Transfer
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

|service| charges $0.09 per GB for all egress data transfer.
