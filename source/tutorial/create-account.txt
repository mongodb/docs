=================
Create an Account
=================

.. meta::
   :description: Create a MongoDB Atlas for Government account by purchasing a subscription or accepting an invitation, and follow steps to register and log in.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

To create a |cloudgov| account, you must purchase a subscription or be 
invited by an active user. Invitations are sent by email.

You can purchase a |cloudgov| subscription from:

- The |aws| Marketplace
- The Google Cloud Marketplace
- :website:`MongoDB Sales </contact/atlas>`
