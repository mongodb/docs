==================================
Open a Saved Pipeline in |service|
==================================

.. facet:: 
   :name: genre
   :values: tutorial

.. meta::
   :description: Load and edit saved aggregation pipelines in Atlas using the pipeline builder.

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can create new pipelines based on previously saved aggregation pipelines. 
Saved aggregation pipelines can be loaded and edited in |service| to build 
new pipelines.

Before You Begin 
----------------

If you don't have a saved pipeline, you must first: 

- :ref:`Create an aggregation pipeline <atlas-ui-create-agg-pipeline>`.

- :ref:`Save your aggregation pipeline <atlas-ui-save-agg-pipeline>`.

Steps
-----

.. procedure:: 
   :style: normal

   .. step:: Click the folder :icon-lg:`Folder` icon.

      In the top-left corner of the pipeline builder, click the 
      :icon-lg:`Folder` folder icon to open the drop-down menu of saved 
      pipelines.
         
   .. step:: Select an aggregation pipeline.
      
      In the drop-down menu, hover over the pipeline you want to open and 
      click :guilabel:`Open`.  
         
   .. step:: Confirm your selection.
      
      In the pop-up modal, click :guilabel:`Open Pipeline`.

   To learn more, see 
   :ref:`viewing saved aggregations <atlas-ui-saved-query-view>` for details.
