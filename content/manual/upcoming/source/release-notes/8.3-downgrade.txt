Feature Compatibility Version and Downgrade
-------------------------------------------

Starting with MongoDB 8.3, the ``setFeatureCompatibilityVersion``
supports downgrading the |fcv| from 8.3 to 8.2.

Follow the |fcv| downgrade steps in each procedure to ensure your
deployment runs with a feature compatibility version that is
supported by the target MongoDB version.
