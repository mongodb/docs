About MongoDB

MongoDB empowers innovators to create, transform, and disrupt industries by unleashing the power of software and data.

Join UsPress Room

Our journey

Dwight Merriman, Eliot Horowitz, and Kevin Ryan — veterans of the online advertising company Doubleclick (acquired by Google) — quickly learned the limitations of relational databases when scaling to serve more than 400,000 ads per second. Seeking to create a more scalable and flexible solution, they founded MongoDB (formerly 10gen) in 2007.
