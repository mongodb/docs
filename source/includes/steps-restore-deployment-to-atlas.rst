.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-continuous-backup.rst
      
   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Overview` tab.
      
   .. step:: Click the ellipses icon to the right of the source deployment and select :guilabel:`Restore`.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Choose Cluster to Restore to`.
      
   .. step:: In the :guilabel:`Project` list, choose the |service-short| project that contains the Atlas deployment.
      
   .. step:: In the :guilabel:`Choose to Restore to` list, choose the |service-short| deployment to which you want to restore.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Restore`.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Confirm & Continue`.
      
      Verify your |mms| password.
