.. _data-explorer-docs:

=================================
Manage Documents in Data Explorer
=================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can use the :guilabel:`Data Explorer` to manage documents inside
your cluster collections. Documents are individual records in a MongoDB
collection and are the basic unit of data in MongoDB.

Viewing documents and collections in the :guilabel:`Data Explorer` can
provide a high-level overview of your database schema. You can use the
:guilabel:`Data Explorer` to ensure you are following MongoDB's core
data modeling concepts, such as utilizing embedded documents and arrays.

.. seealso::

   :manual:`Data Model Design </core/data-model-design/>`

.. include:: /includes/fact-access-de.rst

Required Roles
--------------

To :ref:`insert <de-insert-documents>`, :ref:`edit <de-edit-document>`,
or :ref:`delete documents <de-delete-document>`, you must have been
granted access through one of the following roles:

- :authrole:`Project Owner` or :authrole:`Organization Owner`

- :authrole:`Project Data Access Admin`

- :authrole:`Project Data Access Read/Write`
