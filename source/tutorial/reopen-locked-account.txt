=============================
Reopen a Locked Group Account
=============================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

Overview
--------

|mms| will lock a group's account if the associated credit card expires.

Reopen an Account
-----------------

If you have received a message that your account is locked for failure
to pay, please update your credit card information.

1. Click the :guilabel:`Settings` tab, then click :guilabel:`Billing/Subscriptions`.

2. In the :guilabel:`Payment Methods` section, click the pencil icon for your credit card.

3. Edit information as needed and click :guilabel:`Save Changes`.
