Introduction
------------

libbson builds, parses, and iterates `BSON <http://bsonspec.org>`_ documents, the native data format of
MongoDB. It also converts BSON to and from JSON, and provides a platform compatibility layer for
:ref:`the MongoDB C Driver <index>`.
