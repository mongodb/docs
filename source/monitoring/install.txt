========================
Getting Started with MMS
========================

Installing and configuring the |mms| (MMS) is quick and easy.

.. only:: hosted

   This manual will explain how to install and configure 
   your on-prem monitoring server, and guide you 
   through the MMS installation process specific to your operating 
   system. Other pages will help you to customize your configuration, 
   keep the Agent updated, and provide you with some troubleshooting 
   strategies. 
   
.. only:: saas

   This manual will guide you 
   through the installation process specific to your operating system,
   Other pages will help you customize your configuration, 
   keep the Agent updated, and provide you with some troubleshooting 
   strategies. 

As an alternative, you may follow the install process from
start to finish in the :doc:`/monitoring/tutorial`.
