.. setting:: brs.snapshotSchedule.retention.weekly

   *Type*: integer

   *Default*: 2

   
   Specifies how many weeks a weekly snapshot is stored.
   
   Accepted values are:
   
   ``0``, ``1``, ``2``, ``3``, ``4``, ``5``, ``6``, ``7``, ``8``,
   ``12``, ``16``, ``20``, ``24``, and ``52``
   
   
   Corresponds to ``.Weekly Retention of Snapshots``
