.. _ak8so-monitoring:

===========================================
Monitor Your Atlas {+Database-Deployments+}
===========================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 2
   :class: singlecol

You can use |ak8so| to monitor your |service| {+database-deployments+} 
with the following features.

Project Alerts
--------------

You can use |ak8so| to configure alerts to help you monitor access to 
and the state of the {+database-deployments+} in your |service| 
projects.

To learn more, see :ref:`ak8so-alert-configurations`.

Audit Logs
----------

You can use |ak8so| to configure audit logs.

To learn more, see :ref:`ak8so-configure-audit-logs`.

Third-Party Services
--------------------

You can use |ak8so| to integrate |service| with third-party services to:

- Receive |service| alerts in various third-party services.
- View and analyze :ref:`performance metrics <monitor-cluster-metrics>` 
  that |service| collects about your {+cluster+}.

To learn more, see :ref:`ak8so-integrate-third-party`.
