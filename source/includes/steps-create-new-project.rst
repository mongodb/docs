.. procedure::
   :style: normal

   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-org-projects.rst
      
   .. step:: Go to the :guilabel:`Create a Project` page.
      
      Do one of the following steps:

      - Click :guilabel:`New Project`.
      
      - Expand the :guilabel:`Projects` menu in the navigation
        bar, then click :guilabel:`+ New Project`.
      
   .. step:: Name your new project.
      
      a. Enter the name for your new project.
      
         .. important::
      
            The project name has the following limits:
      
            - Can't exceed 64 characters.
            - Restricted to letters, numbers, spaces, dashes, and
              underscores.
      
            Don't include :ref:`sensitive information <sensitive-info>` in 
            your project name.
      
      b. Click :guilabel:`Next`.
      
   .. step:: Add members.

      a. For existing |service| users, enter their username. Usually, this
         is the email the person used to register.
      
      b. For new |service| users, enter their email address to send an
         invitation.
      
   .. step:: Specify the access for the members.
      
   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Create Project`.
