=================
Encrypt Snapshots
=================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

MongoDB supports encryption on persistent storage for any backup job that uses WiredTiger. An encryption key encrypts the backups when the snapshots are created.
