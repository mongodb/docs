After you create :ref:`mapping rules <create-rule-relational>` for your project, you 
can view, edit, or delete those rules.

Use Cases
---------

Use mapping rules to define how Relational Migrator maps relational
tables and columns to MongoDB collections.
Managing rules allows you to adjust your schema design before you 
migrate data.
