Overview
--------

MongoDB supports multiple mechanisms that you can use to secure your
application. To learn more about how to secure your application, see the
following sections:

- Learn how to use different authentication mechanisms to secure your data in
  the :ref:`Authentication Mechanisms <java-rs-auth>` guide.
- Learn how to encrypt specific data fields in the :ref:`Client-Side Encryption
  <javars-encrypt>` guide.
