====================================
Verify Your Database and Collections
====================================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

*Estimated completion time: 2 minutes*

When you first connect to your {+fdi+}, |service| maps sample data 
stored in the |s3| buckets to a set of databases and collections. This 
part of the tutorial walks you through verifying your databases and 
collections. 

Prerequisites
-------------

To complete this part of the tutorial, you will need to have completed:

- **Part 1:** :doc:`/data-federation/tutorial/deploy`
- **Part 2:** :ref:`config-connection`
- **Part 3:** :doc:`/data-federation/tutorial/connect`
