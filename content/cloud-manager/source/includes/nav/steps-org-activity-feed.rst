.. step:: In |cloud|, go to the :guilabel:`Activity Feed` page for your organization.

   a. If it's not already displayed, select your desired organization 
      from the |ui-org-menu| in the navigation bar.

   #. In the sidebar, click :guilabel:`Activity Feed` under the 
      :guilabel:`Configurations` heading.

   The `Organization Activity Feed <https://cloud.mongodb.com/go?l=https%3A%2F%2Fcloud.mongodb.com%2Fv2%23%2Forg%2F%3Corganization%3E%2Factivity%2Flist>`__ page 
   displays.
