========================
Manage Cluster Lifecycle
========================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

Keeping up to date with the latest MongoDB and |service| features is
essential for maximizing your cluster's security and performance. The
following procedures illustrate best practices for managing your
cluster's lifecycle.

:doc:`/tutorial/major-version-change`
   Create a staging cluster to test your application before upgrading your cluster to a new MongoDB version.

:doc:`/tutorial/cluster-maintenance-window`
   Configure a preferred window for cluster maintenance to minimize impact to your application.
