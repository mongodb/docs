.. procedure::
   :style: normal
      
   .. step:: (Optional) In |service|, create an organization to configure as a paying organization.

      If you wish to create a new organization through which to pay, 
      :ref:`Create an Organization <create-organization>`.
      
      .. note::

         You can :ref:`rename an organization <rename-organization>` to 
         reach your desired cross-organization configuration.
      
   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-org-billing-linked.rst
      
   .. step:: Start linking.

      If you are configuring a new paying organization, click 
      :guilabel:`Start Linking`.
      
      If your organization is already a paying organization, click 
      :guilabel:`Link More Organizations`.
      
   .. step:: Select organizations to link to your paying organization.

      A dialog box displays your selected organization under 
      :guilabel:`Paying organization` on the left and a list of 
      organizations you may link to it under 
      :guilabel:`Select organizations to link` on the right.
      
      Select each organization you wish to link to your 
      paying organization.
      
      Click :guilabel:`Review and Finish`.
      
   .. step:: Review and finalize your links.

      Confirm that your organizations are linked as intended and click 
      :guilabel:`Finish`.
