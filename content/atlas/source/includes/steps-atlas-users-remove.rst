.. procedure::
   :style: normal
      
   .. include:: /includes/nav/steps-project-access-manager.rst
      
   .. step:: Click the :guilabel:`Users` or :guilabel:`Teams` tab.
      
   .. step:: Remove the |service| user or team.

      Click :icon:`trash-alt` to the right of the |service| user or
      team you want to remove.
      
   .. step:: Confirm the removal.
      
      - To confirm |a-service| user removal, click
        :guilabel:`Remove User`.
      
      - To confirm a team removal, click
        :guilabel:`Delete Team`.
      
      - To keep the |service| user or team in the project, click 
        :guilabel:`Cancel`.
