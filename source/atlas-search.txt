.. _atlas-search:

============
Atlas Search
============

.. meta::
   :description: Explore advanced text indexing with Atlas Search, available in Atlas for Government, to enhance search functionality without extra management.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

MongoDB's |service| Search feature offers fine-grained text indexing to 
enable advanced search functionality without any additional management. 
|service| Search is fully available in |cloudgov|.

See the |service| documentation to configure 
:atlas:`Atlas Search </atlas-search/>` indexes.
