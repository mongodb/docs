   :style: normal

   .. step:: Open query history.

      Click the clock :icon-lg:`Clock` icon on the query bar in the 
      :guilabel:`Documents` pane. 

   .. step:: Select favorites.

      Hover over your query and click the star :guilabel:`Star` button.

   .. step:: Name your query.

      Enter a name for your query.

   .. step:: Click :guilabel:`Save`.
