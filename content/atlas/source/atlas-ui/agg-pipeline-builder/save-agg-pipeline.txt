============================
Save a Pipeline in |service|
============================

.. facet:: 
   :name: genre
   :values: tutorial

.. meta::
   :description: Save your aggregation pipelines in Atlas for future use and edit them without altering the original version.

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

You can save your aggregation pipelines to find and use them again later. If 
you load a saved pipeline, you can make edits to the pipeline stages without 
changing the original saved pipeline. You can also create a :ref:`view 
<atlas-ui-view-details>` from your pipeline results.
