====================
MongoDB Search
====================

.. meta::
   :description: Explore advanced text indexing with MongoDB Search, available in Atlas for Government, to enhance search functionality without extra management.

.. default-domain:: mongodb

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

|fts| offers fine-grained text indexing to enable advanced search functionality without
any additional management. |fts| is fully available in |cloudgov|.

See the |service| documentation to configure :atlas:`MongoDB Search </atlas-search/>`
indexes.
