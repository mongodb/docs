Overview
--------

{+adl+} is MongoDB's solution for storing and querying historical data 
from your |service| cluster in a performant analytic storage service.

The following pages guide you through the process of creating and 
connecting to a sample {+dl+} in |service| and running MongoDB Query 
Language (MQL) operations against the data in your {+dl+}. 

- **Step 1:** :ref:`adl-add-pipeline`.
- **Step 2:** :ref:`adl-create-federated-db`.
- **Step 3:** :ref:`adl-connect-federated-db-instance`.
- **Step 4:** :ref:`adl-run-queries`.
