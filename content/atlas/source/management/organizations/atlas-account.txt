=============================
Manage Your |service| Account
=============================

.. meta::
   :keywords: atlas
   :description: Manage your Atlas account settings, update your contact information, configure security preferences, and personalize the Atlas UI for your team's workflow.

.. contents:: On this page
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1
   :class: singlecol

Your |service| account controls access to all |service| projects
and organizations you belong to. From your account, you can
manage your personal settings, update your contact information,
and customize the {+atlas-ui+} to fit your workflow. To manage
your |service| account, see:

- :ref:`manage-your-mongodb-atlas-account`
- :ref:`atlas-personalization`
